Choosing a School

Choosing a martial art and school:

With all of the different martial arts styles available it can be overwhelming when searching for the style and school that meets your needs. Here are some tips to help you decide.

  1. Decide what you want to get out of the training.
    1. Self-defense
    2. Fitness
    3. Competition
    4. Comradery
  2. Look for a school that offers adult classes and/or family classes if you wish to train with your child(ren).
  3. Trial a school, most schools offer a trial period before you commit. This is important. If you are not satisfied with the training, change schools. (I have trained under five grand masters. Not all schools are the same.)
  4. Speak with the master about your goals, limitations and responsibilities outside of training. They will be able to help in meeting your needs.
